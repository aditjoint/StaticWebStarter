Utkal Electrotechnic

Strategic Partner

Utkal Electrotechnic

Focus: Electricity Distribution, Power Conversion & Metering, Project Development

About:

As a strategic partner, Utkal Electrotechnic supports turnkey execution, project structuring, and local compliance in international markets.

M/S Electrotechnic focuses are on Project Management and consultancy; they specialize in delivering high-performance Transformer and Metering solutions across industrial, commercial, and utility sectors — from design and manufacturing to deployment, calibration, and handover.

With a strong focus on efficiency, accuracy, and grid reliability, sourcing includes:

  • Distribution and Power Transformers
  • Energy Meters (AMR/AMI enabled, Smart Meters, Net Meters)
  • Metering Infrastructure & Data Analytics Solutions
  • Transformer Monitoring & Diagnostic Systems

Backed by a team of skilled engineers and industry-certified facilities, they ensure compliance with IS/IEC/ANSI standards, while integrating digital technologies for real-time monitoring and remote asset management.

Key Contributions:

  • EPC & project deployment support
  • Local market enablement in Nigeria
  • Industrial design, tendering, and compliance
  • Global engineering alliances
