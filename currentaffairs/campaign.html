KMG Australia

Strategic Partner

kmgaustralia.com

Focus: Engineering, Procurement & Project Development

About:

As a strategic partner, KMG supports turnkey execution, project structuring, and local compliance in Australia and international markets. KMG focuses are on the following domains namely – Project Management and consultancy; Renewable Energy (Waste to energy – Bio gas/Solid organic fertilizer/Liquid organic fertilizer/Carbon dioxide/ Distilled water/Electricity/bio CNG gas for transport) , solar, Electric Vehicle, Storage systems and batteries); Defense and Security (Defense projects, Defense international tie ups, Defense innovation and supply chain opportunities for international companies), IT areas including Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Augmented Reality/Virtual Reality, Cyber Security and Protection (Cyber projects, Cyber training and Manpower); Agriculture (unmanned vehicles and drones for soil mapping, plant maintenance, Plant protection and pest control), Bio fuel cultivation.

Key Contributions:

  • EPC & project deployment support
  • Local market enablement in Australia
  • Industrial design, tendering, and compliance
  • Global engineering alliances

KMGX Energy Storage Systems

Technology Partner

KMGXx.com

Focus: Advanced Lithium Titanate (LTO) Battery Technology

About:

KMGX is an Australian innovator in sustainable energy storage solutions, specializing in high-performance Lithium Titanate (LTO) battery systems. Their battery packs offer exceptional safety, longevity (up to 20,000 cycles), and efficiency for residential, commercial, and grid applications.

Key Contributions:

  • LTO battery cell IP and design licensing
  • Technical co-development and product certification
  • Support for UN38.3, IEC, BIS compliance
  • Demo units, warranty structure, and pilot support
